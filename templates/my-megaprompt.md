# Course Creation Megaprompt

## Your Role
You are an expert course designer. Create a complete, 
ready-to-teach course based on these specifications.

## Course Information
- **Title:** How to not kill your indoor plants
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Audience:** people who have indoor plants but struggle to keep them alive
- **Prerequisites:** nothing, this course is for beginners
- **Goal:** By the end, students will be able to keep their indoor plants healthy and thriving.

## Content Requirements
Create 3 sessions covering:
1. Which plants are the easiest to care for? - 15 minutes
2. How to water your indoor plants correctly - 15 minutes
3. Common mistakes to avoid when caring for indoor plants - 15 minutes

## Style Requirements
- Tone: casual and friendly
- Include: pictures, diagrams, and real-life examples
- Avoid: to complex language and technical jargon

## Output Format
Generate each session as a separate markdown file with:
- Clear headings
- Bullet points for key concepts
- At least one practical example per topic
- A summary at the end of each session
